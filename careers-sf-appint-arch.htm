Your Career with Pierce Washington

Application and Integration Architect

San Francisco

Pierce Washington is a full service eBusiness consulting firm, with offices in San Francisco, Chicago and London.

Role

Pierce Washington LLC seeks Application and Integration Architect in San Francisco, CA. (and other U.S. locations as needed)

Job Duties

Effectively manages integration between multiple outside systems including the use of middleware applications. Conducts performance tuning and diagnose/resolve obscure technical compatibility issues. Translate business and technical requirements into an overall solution that is robust, scalable, and extensible. Effectively implements and manages multiple environments to support project needs (e.g. dev, UAT, prod).

Requires a Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems/Technology or a related field, plus 4 years of experience. Experience with complex integration and security issues. Knowledge of ERP systems and integration tools. Experience in HTML, Java, XML, XSL, CSS, JQuery, and Object Oriented Programing. Ability to weigh the costs, benefits, risks and chances for success and to provide estimates on how long it will take to complete development work. Ability to lead technical discussions in front of audience within the Oracle CPQ application. Must be willing to travel to unanticipated client sites as needed. 40 hours/week.

Must also have authority to work permanently in the U.S.
