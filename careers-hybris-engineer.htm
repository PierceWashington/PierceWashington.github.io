Hybris Engineer

Senior Level

San Francisco or Chicago

Pierce Washington is a full service eBusiness consulting firm, with offices in San Francisco and Chicago.

Role

We are looking for an experienced hybris engineer for a fulltime position in either our San Francisco or Chicago offices. This individual will work collaboratively to scope and implement solutions, manage day to day troubleshooting of the platform and make architecture related decisions on bugs, enhancements and implementations.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Responsible for the technical design of hybris client solutions, including participation in strategy, analysis, design, and implementation.
  • Assists the Project Manager in establishing project plan and budget.
  • Effectively communicate and mentor customers and developers as they learn to implement hybris.
  • Contribute to continual process and methodology improvements.
  • Provide sales support on new and existing business initiatives.

Required Skills:

  • Five to eight years of related experience. Previous hybris project experience a plus.
  • BS or MS degree in Computer Science or Engineering from a reputable four‚Äêyear university.
  • Strong critical thinking, problem solving and analytical skills, as well as verbal and written communication skills.
  • Experienced with web technologies, application servers, portal servers, middleware, eCommerce, enterprise application integration, databases, security, performance and scalability.
  • Experience with and deep understanding of the following technologies: Java, Spring framework, Eclipse, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS stylesheet, XML, Python, XSLT, XPATH, SOAP, SQL and relational database concepts.
  • The ability to quickly learn new skills and technologies and convert them into client solutions.
  • Complete understanding of the full software development lifecycle.
  • Direct customer facing and implementation delivery experience in a consulting or professional services organization.
  • Desire to work in a fast paced, growing and deadline driven environment.
  • Strong collaboration skills and the ability to mentor and be mentored.
  • Ability to meet client travel requirements (up to 20%).
