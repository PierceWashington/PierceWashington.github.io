Your Career with Pierce Washington

eCommerce Implementation Engineer

Senior Level

Chicago

Pierce Washington is a full service eBusiness consulting firm, with offices in San Francisco and Chicago.

Role

We are looking for an experienced Senior eCommerce Implementation Engineer for a fulltime position in our Chicago office. Under general direction from one of our Application Architects, this individual will work closely with fellow team members to rapidly build and deploy large online eCommerce solutions. For small projects, this individual must be comfortable managing the majority of the project through its lifecycle.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Manage complex, web-based technology projects through all phases of the project lifecycle, from project definition through implementation.
  • Assist with the functional documentation of customer business requirements.
  • Translate business requirements into a technical solution.
  • Configure and script software application based on technical design solution.
  • Work closely with project manager to identify in a proactive manner any potential issues and risks.
  • Coach and mentor customers and junior team members.

Required Skills:

  • At a minimum, four years of related experience.
  • BS or MS degree in Computer Science or Engineering from a reputable four-year university.
  • Strong critical thinking, problem solving and analytical skills, as well as verbal and written communication skills.
  • Experience and comfort with the following technologies: HTML, XML, Java, JavaScript, Python, XSLT, XPATH, CSS stylesheet, jQuery, SOAP and relational database concepts and SQL.
  • The ability to quickly learn new concepts and technologies and convert them into customer solutions.
  • Direct customer facing and implementation delivery experience in a Consulting or Professional Services organization.
  • Experience with web technologies, application servers, portal servers, middleware, eCommerce, enterprise application integration, databases, security, performance and scalability.
  • Desire to work in a fast paced, growing and deadline driven environment.
  • Complete understanding of the full product development lifecycle (design, development and testing).
  • Strong collaboration skills, ability to mentor and be mentored.
  • Ability to meet client travel requirements (up to 20%).

To Apply:

Please send your resume with cover letter, references and salary requirements to recruiting@piercewashington.com.
